XPanda Ops Platform

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 1, 2026  ·  XPanda Foam, LLC

This Privacy Policy describes how XPanda Foam, LLC ("XPanda," "we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the XPanda Ops Platform and its QuickBooks Online integration.

1. Scope

The XPanda Ops Platform is an internal business application used exclusively by XPanda Foam employees and authorized contractors. It is not a public-facing product or service offered to third parties.

2. Information We Access from QuickBooks Online

With your authorization via Intuit's OAuth 2.0 flow, the XPanda Ops Platform reads the following data from your connected QuickBooks Online company file:

  • Customer name and reference identifiers
  • Invoice numbers and transaction dates
  • Billing and shipping addresses
  • Line item details (item names, descriptions, quantities, and amounts)

We do not access financial account credentials, payment card numbers, bank account information, or any data beyond what is necessary to create and manage production and shipping jobs.

3. How We Use This Information

Data retrieved from QuickBooks Online is used solely to:

  • Create job records in the XPanda Ops Platform corresponding to QBO invoices
  • Pre-populate shipping address and line item details for production workflow
  • Display job and customer information to authorized XPanda personnel

We do not use this data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose unrelated to XPanda's internal operations.

4. Data Storage and Security

Retrieved invoice data is stored in XPanda's internal database hosted on Cloudflare's infrastructure. OAuth access tokens are stored securely and are used only to authenticate API requests on your behalf. Refresh tokens are used solely to maintain continuous access without requiring repeated manual authorization.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect this data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share data retrieved from QuickBooks Online with any third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to operate the application (e.g., our hosting provider, Cloudflare).

6. Data Retention

Job records created from QBO invoice data are retained for as long as necessary to support XPanda's operational and record-keeping requirements. OAuth tokens are overwritten or deleted when a connection is revoked or re-authorized.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke the XPanda Ops Platform's access to your QuickBooks Online data at any time through your Intuit account settings. Revocation will prevent the application from retrieving new invoice data but will not automatically delete job records already created.

8. Intuit's Privacy Practices

Data handled by Intuit in connection with QuickBooks Online is governed by Intuit's Privacy Statement. XPanda is not responsible for Intuit's privacy practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page reflects the date of the most recent revision. Continued use of the application following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact XPanda Foam, LLC via the contact information on file with Intuit's developer portal.
